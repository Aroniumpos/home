FAQ

A nice place to start

Find the most frequently asked questions below.

For everything you wanted to know about Aronium POS, but you could not find it here, feel free to contact our support at support@aronium.com, sales representatives at sales@aronium.com, or, simply, drop us a line using our contact form.

We are happy to answer any questions you may have.
1. Is it really free?
Yes it is. You can use free Aronium version as long as you like. There are no obligations or hidden costs.

However, keep in mind that Aronium is a "freemium" product, meaning that there is a free version, and other paid versions or extensions offered as more advanced alternative.
Of course, everything you downloaded for free, will remain free forever, and we'll do our best to add as much free features as possible.

To be sure what options are free of charge, look for the FREE sign on our download page.
All other, paid options, will be clearly marked as PAID
2. How to buy a license?

License purchase is done on My Aronium via PayPal and it takes only a few minutes. All payment processing is done directly on PayPal.

For detailed instructions how to buy a license, please see this article on our help center.

If you have any questions related to your license or license purchase, feel free to reach out to sales@aronium.com anytime.

3. What are the minimum hardware requirements for Aronium POS?
Long story short: if you are using Windows 7, 8/8.1 or Windows 10, you will be able to run and use Aronium on your PC.
Aronium works with any standard PC configuration that includes mouse and keyboard, and it is compatible and designed to support touch screen monitors, too.

If you are still interested in technical details, our recommendation is:
  • Processor: 1 GHz or higher recommended
  • Memory: 1 GB of RAM (32-bit) or 2 GB (64-bit)
  • Hard Disk Space: At least 50 MB of available free space
  • Monitor: VGA 1024x768 or higher-resolution
4. Does Aronium work in my country?
Aronium is created as customizable application, meaning that you define all required assets and properties for your business, including taxes and payment types.
If you, anyway, find an obstacle in using Aronium, please, feel free to contact us anytime, we will be glad to help and make it work in your country.
5. Is internet connection required?
No, it is not required, but we recommend it.
Aronium is an offline application, meaning it works without internet connection, unless you are using modules that are directly dependent on internet based applications.

We recommend internet connection to make sure you have the latest version installed, but, if internet connection is not available, you can always transfer updates to your POS on a flash drive, once downloaded from a location with internet connection.
6. How much does it take to set up Aronium?
Installation takes only couple of minutes, but your store setup actually depends on how many products you have.
Basically, a typical store with approximately 100 products can be set in couple of hours, as creating a product takes only a minute, once you are familiar with it.
7. Which versions of Windows are supported?
Aronium supports all major Windows versions, including Windows 7, Windows 8+ and Windows 10.
With latest operating system updates, Aronium is compatible with Windows XP, but, if you still use Windows XP, we recommend upgrading to newer Windows versions for your security and optimizations.
